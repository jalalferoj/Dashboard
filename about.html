About the Enhanced Service Dashboard

What is this?

This dashboard is a powerful web-based tool for analyzing service ticket data. It allows you to upload your data in various formats (CSV, TXT, Excel) and instantly generate interactive charts and visualizations. The goal is to help you understand patterns in your service requests, track performance metrics, and make data-driven decisions.

Features
  • Multi-format Support: Upload CSV, TXT, or Excel (.xlsx) files.
  • Interactive Charts: 11 different chart types for comprehensive data analysis.
  • Dark Theme: Modern, easy-on-the-eyes interface.
  • Responsive Design: Works on desktop and mobile devices.
  • Real-time Processing: Instant chart generation from uploaded data.
  • Client-Side Processing: All data is processed in your browser, ensuring privacy and security. No data is sent to a server.
How to Use
  1. Go to the Dashboard page.
  2. Click "Choose File" or drag and drop your data file.
  3. The dashboard will automatically process your data and display the charts.
